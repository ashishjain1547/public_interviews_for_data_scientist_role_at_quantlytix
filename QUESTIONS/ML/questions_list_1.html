Share some questions to ask in an ML interview.

Absolutely! Here’s a mix of technical, theoretical, and practical machine learning interview questions—good to ask the interviewer or be prepared to answer, depending on the context:

🔍 To Ask the Interviewer (to show curiosity & depth)

📌 About the Role / Team

  • What are the most common machine learning problems your team tackles?

  • Which algorithms or models are used most frequently in your production systems?

  • How much of the work is modeling vs. data preprocessing / feature engineering?

  • How do you handle model monitoring and performance degradation over time?

  • Is the ML pipeline fully automated or more manual? What tools are used (e.g., MLFlow, Airflow, etc.)?

📌 About Data & Collaboration

  • What kind of datasets does the team typically work with (structured, unstructured, time-series, etc.)?

  • Are there any major data quality or imbalance issues you face often?

  • How do ML engineers and data scientists collaborate with software engineers or product teams?

📌 About the ML Lifecycle

  • What’s your typical approach to model selection and evaluation?

  • How do you manage hyperparameter tuning and experimentation at scale?

  • How is feedback from deployed models used to improve future versions?

📌 About Career Growth / Learning

  • Are there opportunities for research or publishing in this role?

  • How do team members stay current with the latest in ML/AI?

💡 To Be Prepared to Answer

(These are often asked in technical rounds or behavioral interviews.)

📊 Core ML & Statistics

  • How do you choose between bias and variance in model training?

  • What is regularization? Explain L1 vs. L2.

  • Explain precision, recall, F1-score, and when you’d prioritize one over the other.

  • What is the ROC curve? How is AUC useful?

🧠 Modeling & Algorithms

  • When would you prefer decision trees over logistic regression?

  • Explain how a random forest handles overfitting.

  • What are the assumptions behind linear regression?

  • Describe the differences between bagging and boosting.

🧹 Data Handling

  • How do you handle missing data?

  • What techniques do you use for feature selection?

  • How do you treat categorical variables for ML models?

⚙️ Deployment & Production

  • How would you deploy a model for real-time inference?

  • What steps would you take if your deployed model’s accuracy starts dropping?

  • How do you track and compare model performance over time?

🧠 Deep Learning (if relevant)

  • What’s the difference between a CNN and an RNN?

  • When would you use transfer learning?

  • How do activation functions affect the learning process?
